FFM: Game strategy

Initial Writeup
  1. There are a total of 52 cards in play
  2. Every Market Maker is given 5 cards
  3. The speculator only has 3 cards (Stronger in the endgame?)
  4. The remaining 29 cards are revealed one at a time after which the players can bid on a suit
  5. The suit at the bottom pays out contracts:
    • All contracts are worth £1
    • Contracts that didn't pay out (other suits) are worth £0
  6. Win condition? (Get the most money...)
  7. The game seems more behavioural

For each suit, at any given point in time the probability of it being at the bottom is (13 - revealed - hidden_in_hand) / (52 - revealed)
